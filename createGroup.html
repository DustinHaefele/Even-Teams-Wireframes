How to add players

It's pretty simple. You told us how many players you have, now type in their names and given them a skill level. 1 is the lowest and 100 is the highest! Once you have filled out the information for all the players just hit submit and your group is done. If you need to add more players or make any changes don't worry you can do that in a little bit.
