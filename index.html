About Even Teams

Have you ever had to be a captain for a pick up game of soccer? Or have you ever organized a single person sign up tournament? Than you know how hard it can be to make teams as even as possible. No one wants to come out and play a friendly game of volleyball on a wednesday night and lose 25-10. That's where we come in. We take the best even split algorithm available and put it to work to make your teams as even as scientifically possible!

How to use the App

To use the app, create a new group below. You tell us how many players you have. Fill out the names and skill levels of each player and let us create as many teams as you need in the most even way possible.
